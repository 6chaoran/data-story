BMI prediction from Webcam / Uploaded Image using Tensorflow.js

For Webcam:

  1. click [Enable Webcam] button and place your face in the red box, the prediction readings will be displayed at the bottom.
  2. click [Stop Webcam] button to stop the prediction, or wait for 5 seconds to capture the readings.

For Uploaded Image:

  1. click [Upload Image] to choose an image from your local disk.
  2. click [Predict] to get the results.

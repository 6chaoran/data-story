You're likely using a mobile device, where the webcam prediction is not working properly. At least I tested on my iPhone X, which is not working. Please switch to a desktop browser, if you are keen to test the webcam prediction.

For Webcam:

  1. click [Enable Webcam] button and place your face in the red box, the prediction readings will be displayed at the bottom.
  2. click [Stop Webcam] button to stop the prediction, or wait for 5 seconds to automatically capture the readings.

For Uploaded Image:

  1. click [Upload Image] to choose an image from your local disk.
  2. click [Predict] to get the results.
